The Sanitisers

The Sanitisers was established in March 2020 as a direct consequence of COVID-19. Harry Hynes and Damian Morgan recognised that there was a need for a service to help business owners reopen and maintain their businesses in an environment that was as COVID-19 safe as possible. Sanitising business premises and offices on a regular basis will give customers and employees confidence and peace of mind while they go about their activities. We at The Sanitisers work with each individual business to identify their specific needs and develop a sanitisation plan accordingly. We work effectively with a large cross section of the business and social communities, sanitising a diverse range of premises including offices, shops, pubs, restaurants, cinemas, schools, churches and community halls.

Damian Morgan

Damian Morgan has been involved in the service industry for over 30 years. During this period, he developed, owned and managed Captain Morgan’s Pub Group. He is currently part owner/director of G.I.F.T. Ltd (Growing Independent Free trade) who are consultants to the licence trade. He is also part owner/director of Music Venue Connect Ltd a company that organises music and other entertainment for pubs throughout Munster. He has developed an extensive knowledge of the standards that need to be met within the service industry and looks forward to working with businesses to reach and maintain these high standards.

Harry Hynes

Harry Hynes has over 35 years’ experience in the service industry in both Ireland and the UK. He has owned and managed a number of licenced premises in London and on his return to Ireland became the owner of Hynes Quick Pick retail business in Tralee. Having managed Austin Stacks GAA Club bar/function room he currently owns and runs Oceans Casino in Tralee, Co Kerry. Being a long-time employer and active participant in the service industry he is aware of the standards of hygiene required to run a successful business. He is using his vast knowledge and experience to assist businesses to obtain a consistently high standard of hygiene in order to successfully negotiate these difficult times.
