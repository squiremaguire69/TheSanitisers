Our Cleaning Process

Bio-fogging is known to be a safe and effective way of sanitising interior areas of premises. Fogging is a method where a high-speed motor atomises a chemical producing a fine mist of disinfectant which is dispersed into the air. These micro-droplets of sanitisation fluid find their way into areas such as cracks and crevices that could not otherwise be reached by conventional cleaning methods.

We use a certified cleaning agent within this process and our operators are fully suited in PPE (Personal Protective Equipment).

  • Areas must be cleaned before fogging.
  • Protective Equipment should be worn when conducting fogging.
  • Area should be left for at least 30mins after fogging application to allow to dry.
  • Fogging adds a protective layer to surfaces against multiple sources of infection.
  • After fogging airborne infectants can be introduced to the environment and infect persons in the area.
  • Protection is for a limited time only and regular applications are recommended to protect against infection.
